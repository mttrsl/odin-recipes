Philadelphia Chicken Pasta

Description

Philadelphia cheese pasta is a creamy and comforting dish that incorporates Philadelphia cream cheese as the base for its sauce. Known for its smooth texture and mild tang, the cream cheese melts into a luscious, velvety sauce that coats the pasta perfectly. Often paired with garlic, herbs like parsley or basil, and a touch of parmesan for depth, this dish is quick to prepare and appeals to a wide range of tastes.

This versatile recipe allows for endless customization, such as adding saut√©ed vegetables, grilled chicken, or smoked salmon for extra flavor and protein. Its creamy richness makes it a favorite for weeknight dinners or indulgent meals, combining simplicity with a luxurious texture that feels gourmet without requiring advanced cooking skills.

Ingredients

  • 350g of pasta
  • 500g of cooked chicken
  • 50g of Philadelphia cream cheese
  • 50g of Italian hard cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 125ml of chicken stock mixed with water

Method

  1. Bring a large pan of water to boil.
  2. Whilst the water is boiling, finely grate or dice the garlic cloves and chop the cooked chicken breast into chunks.
  3. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook for 10-12 minutes.
  4. In a large frying pan, heat a splash of oil on a medium heat and add the garlic and chicken chunks
  5. Once the garlic has browned slightly, add the 125ml of chicken stock and the Philadelphia cream cheese.
  6. After 5 minutes, or once the cream cheese has fully melted, add the 50g of Italian hard cheese, along with some pepper and italian seasoning.
  7. when the pasta is cooked, add it into the sauce and mix together so everything is coated. Add 3 tablespoons of the pasta water too.
  8. Serve the pasta, and sprinkle with cheese as a garnish.
